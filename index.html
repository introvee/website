About Introvee

Introvee is a gentle confidence-building app that helps users complete small social challenges every day. Each dare is designed to be safe, simple, and beginner-friendly, helping users slowly move outside their comfort zone without pressure.

Key Features

🌱

100-Day Journey

Embark on a guided 100-day path designed specifically for introverts to build social confidence gradually.

⭐

20 Growth Levels

Unlock new emotional growth levels as you complete dares, keeping you motivated and engaged.

🛡️

Safe Daily Dares

Engage in small, respectful, and safe social dares tailored for different comfort zones.

📈

Progress Tracking

Earn points for every completed dare and watch your confidence grow with visual progress charts.

🦊

Cute Mascot Guide

Your friendly mascot is always there to encourage, guide, and support you throughout your journey.

✨

Calm Design

A distraction-free, beautifully soft interface designed to reduce anxiety and promote peace.

How It Works

1

Create your account

Sign up easily and meet your mascot guide who will personalize your 100-day journey.

2

Start your daily dare

Receive a gentle, safe social challenge each day designed for your current level.

3

Complete and earn points

Do the dare at your own pace. Once completed, log it in the app to earn points.

4

Grow confidence slowly

Unlock new levels and watch yourself transform from shy to comfortably confident.

🛡️ Safety & Respect

Introvee is built around safe, respectful, and simple social dares.

  • Users should only complete tasks that feel safe, legal, and respectful to others.
  • Introvee is not a medical or mental health treatment app.
  • Always prioritize your well-being.
Note: Introvee is intended for users aged 16 and above.
