Delete Account

How to permanently remove your data from Introvee.

In-App Deletion (Recommended)

The fastest way to delete your account and all associated data is directly through the Introvee app:

  • Open the Introvee app
  • Go to the Settings screen (gear icon)
  • Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  • Confirm the deletion when prompted

Once confirmed, your account details, progress, and completed dares will be immediately and permanently removed from our database.

Email Request Deletion

If you no longer have access to the app or prefer to request deletion manually, you can send us an email:

  • Send an email to support@introvee.com
  • Ensure you send the request from the same email address you used to register for Introvee.
  • Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"

Once we verify your request, all your account data and progress data will be securely deleted within 5-7 business days. We will notify you once the process is complete.
