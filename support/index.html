Support & Help

We're always here to assist you on your journey.

How to Ask for Help

If you have any questions regarding your dares, account, or general usage of the app, please don't hesitate to reach out to us. We try to respond within 24-48 hours.

Contact Email: support@introvee.com

How to Report Bugs

Found a glitch or something not working quite right? Let us know so we can fix it! When reporting a bug, please include:

  • A brief description of the issue
  • What device you are using (e.g., Android phone model)
  • Any screenshots if applicable

Please send bug reports to the email above.

Payment & Donation Support

If you experience any issues while trying to make a donation via PayPal or UPI, please email us with your transaction details (do not include sensitive payment info like passwords or full card numbers) and we will assist you promptly.

Disabling the Donation Popup

Donations are entirely voluntary. If you prefer not to see the donation prompts, you can easily disable them:

  • Open the Introvee app
  • Go to Settings
  • Toggle the "Show Donation Popup" option to Off

Safety First

Do you have risky dares?
No. Introvee is designed for safe and simple confidence-building tasks. We do not encourage risky, harmful, dangerous, or unsafe activities.

Your safety is more important than any dare, points, streaks, or progress. If a dare feels uncomfortable, unsafe, or not suitable for your situation, you can skip it and choose your safety first.

How are dares made?
Introvee dares are created based on the life category the user chooses, such as confidence, communication, social comfort, self-growth, or daily habits.

Each dare is designed to be simple, safe, and practical for everyday life. The goal is to help users grow slowly without pressure, fear, or risky actions.
