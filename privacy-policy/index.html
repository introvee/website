We use secure authentication methods, including Google Sign-In, to ensure your account is protected. Your login credentials are not stored directly on our servers but are handled by trusted third-party providers.

5. Data security

We take data security seriously. Your personal information is encrypted in transit and at rest. We implement industry-standard security measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction of your data.

You have the right to delete your account and all associated data at any time. You can do this directly within the app settings or by following the instructions on our Delete Account page.

8. Safety First

Do you have risky dares?
No. Introvee is designed for safe and simple confidence-building tasks. We do not encourage risky, harmful, dangerous, or unsafe activities.

Your safety is more important than any dare, points, streaks, or progress. If a dare feels uncomfortable, unsafe, or not suitable for your situation, you can skip it and choose your safety first.

How are dares made?
Introvee dares are created based on the life category the user chooses, such as confidence, communication, social comfort, self-growth, or daily habits.

Each dare is designed to be simple, safe, and practical for everyday life. The goal is to help users grow slowly without pressure, fear, or risky actions.
